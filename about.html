Understanding the Intersection of Population and Flood Risk in Ireland

Ireland faces increasing flood risks due to climate change, with both coastal and inland communities being affected. Our platform explores how population density and settlement patterns overlap with flood-prone areas, offering insights into vulnerable regions and helping inform smarter planning and policy decisions.

  1. Highlight vulnerable population clusters
  2. Visualize current and projected flood zones
  3. Support data-informed decision-making
  4. Promote environmental resilience
